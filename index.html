Our Promises — In Writing

Five Guarantees Every Client Gets Upfront

01
Fixed Price. Period.
Your price is locked in writing before we start. No change orders, no surprise invoices. What we quote is what you pay.
02
Owner On Every Job
Victor is personally on site for every project. Not a project manager — the owner you hired shows up and stays accountable.
03
2-Year Warranty Written
Every project comes with a 2-year workmanship warranty in your contract. If something isn't right, we fix it. No runaround.
04
3D Design Included
You see your project in 3D before a single stone is moved. The planning deposit covers your design and credits 100% toward your project.
05
Owner Accountability Guarantee
We put our timeline in writing. If we miss it without cause, you get credited for any delays outside our control. We stand behind our schedule.
