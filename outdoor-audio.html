Common Questions

Outdoor Audio FAQ

Can outdoor audio be added to an existing patio?
Yes. We install SixFive systems on existing patios and landscapes. We run wiring through conduit or bury it to minimize disruption to your existing hardscape.
How many speakers do I need?
For most Northern Virginia patios (400–800 sq ft), 4 satellite speakers plus a subwoofer is ideal. Larger spaces or multi-zone setups work best with 8 speakers.
Do the speakers hold up in winter?
Yes. SixFive Audio speakers are rated for all-weather outdoor use including freeze-thaw cycles. The solid brass construction handles Virginia's climate without issue.
Can I control it from my phone?
Yes. Add a Sonos Amp, Apple AirPlay receiver, or Bluetooth receiver and you can control everything from your phone wirelessly.
Is it better to install audio during a patio build?
Absolutely — this is the ideal time. Wiring gets buried during construction and speakers are positioned perfectly in the design. One fixed price covers everything.
What warranty comes with the system?
SixFive Audio products carry the manufacturer warranty. P&L provides our standard 2-year workmanship warranty on the installation itself.
